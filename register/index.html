Payment:

Sadly, we do not yet accept online payment. The cost of camp this year is $175 per camper,so please send $175, in either cash or check, with your camper on the day of camp. If your camper is a Junior camper, add on to the fee the amount for their canteen. Generally, $20 is more than enough. Please be reasonable.
Once you have finished registering, please come back to this page, and click the button at the top that says "I've Registered: Now What" to get more information.
Once you've registered, you will recieve an email within 48 hours, confirming your registration and providing additional information. If you do not recieve this email, please contact us as soon as possible. We want to see you at camp and we want to make it as easy for you as possible!
